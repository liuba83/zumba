About

Hi there! My name is Hanna Kovalenko, and I am a licensed Zumba® Instructor, passionate about helping people connect with their bodies, uplift their spirits, and have fun through dance and movement. With certifications in Zumba® Basic 1, Zumba Gold®, and CIRCL Mobility™, I bring a unique blend of expertise to every class.

My expertise is confirmed by licenses:

  • CIRCL Mobility™ Instructor License (January 14, 2023)
  • Zumba® Basic 1 Instructor License (March 3, 2023)
  • Zumba Gold® Instructor License (April 6, 2023)

Why Zumba?

Zumba® is more than just a workout – it’s a way to feel energized, joyful, and in sync with yourself and others. It blends fitness with music and rhythm from around the globe, offering numerous benefits:

  • Boosts Physical Fitness: Improve your cardiovascular health, endurance, and coordination.
  • Reduces Stress: Dance away your worries and leave with a smile.
  • Enhances Mental Well-being: Stay sharp and focused through the power of movement and music.
  • Builds Community: Join a welcoming environment that celebrates every individual.

If you don't know what Zumba is, please visit the official website.

My classes

I specialize in offering Zumba Gold® classes for individuals aged 40 and above, providing a medium intensity yet invigorating experience. Each 30-minute class is a lively journey through various musical styles including merengue, swing, tango, reggae, and more. I also offer Caribbean-style programs with Ska, Soca, and additional rhythms.

I offer both group and private classes tailored to your preferences. For pricing, class schedules, and locations, please visit my official instructor page.

Every class is a party – a Party for Your Heart! Enjoy 8-9 songs that get you moving, smiling, and feeling amazing.

Zumba Gold® Chair

This class offers all the benefits of Zumba® while being seated, making it accessible for everyone. Enjoy the rhythm, movements, and energy tailored to your abilities.

Benefits of Zumba Gold® Chair

  • Improves Mobility: Gentle movements enhance joint flexibility and range of motion.
  • Boosts Mood: Enjoy the music and uplifting environment without worrying about standing.
  • Inclusive Fitness: Designed for all fitness levels, ensuring everyone can participate.
